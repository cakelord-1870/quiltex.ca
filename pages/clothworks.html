Clothworks

Clothworks is family owned and operated and produces outstanding quality fabric for quilters and sewists who love to create. Fresh, fun and full of variety, our fabric speaks for itself. Sourcing designs and art from talented designers within the quilting industry and fine artists from outside the textile world allows Clothworks to produce original collections in every genre that delight and inspire quilters, crafters sand sewers. And with our ever growing Basics lines, you can count on keeping your store or your stash fully stocked with every color in the rainbow.
